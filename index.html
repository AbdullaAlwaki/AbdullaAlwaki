what do i do ?

I design websites that encompass a multitude of devices, delivery methods, and content types. Not only for browsers but for all user experiences. With a combination of HTML , CSS, and JavaScript, including images, streaming video or audio and text, I inevitably get the job done.

Graphic Design

I create visual concepts by hand and computer. I communicate ideas to inspire, inform, or captivate consumers. I help to make an organization recognizable by using a variety of mediums to communicate a particular idea or identity to be used in advertising and promotions.

Instructor

I share my knowledge through online courses in English & Arabic. I am skilled in communicating with students, can explain the decision-making processes behind great work and how to make good choices. I currently guide a large audience of practicing designers.
